# GTM.md

## Target User

The ideal user for StackTrim is a technical founder or engineering manager at a Seed to Series A startup with 5–50 employees and 3–20 active AI tool subscriptions. These teams are moving quickly, experimenting with multiple coding assistants and LLM providers, and often have decentralized purchasing: developers expense tools individually while API usage is billed separately.

The most likely first user is a founder who notices that monthly AI spend has grown from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars without a clear understanding of whether the team is using the most cost-effective plans.

---

## Trigger Moment

The highest-intent trigger moment is when a founder or engineering lead sees:

- Unexpected increases in OpenAI or Anthropic API bills
- Multiple overlapping subscriptions (Cursor, ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot)
- Pressure from investors to reduce burn
- Annual SaaS budget reviews

Right before using StackTrim, they are likely searching for terms such as:

- “How to reduce AI tool costs”
- “Cursor vs GitHub Copilot pricing”
- “Best ChatGPT plan for startup teams”
- “OpenAI API credits”
- “How much should startups spend on AI tools”

## First 100 Users in 30 Days (No Paid Budget)

### 1. Product Hunt and Hacker News Launch
Launch StackTrim as a free “AI Spend Audit” tool with a clear promise: find wasted AI spend in five minutes.

### 2. Founder Outreach
Personally DM 50 founders and engineering leads with a concise message offering a free audit.

### 3. Reddit Participation
Share the tool in relevant discussions about rising AI costs and coding tool comparisons.

### 4. LinkedIn and X Content
Publish short examples of real savings opportunities uncovered by the tool.

### 5. Credex Distribution
Feature the audit prominently on the Credex website and in outbound sales conversations.

Expected result: 100–150 audit completions, 20–30 captured leads, and 5–10 consultation requests.

## Why This GTM Works

StackTrim solves a specific, timely pain point: founders know they are spending more on AI but lack benchmarks and practical recommendations. The product delivers immediate value before asking for contact information, making it naturally shareable and highly aligned with Credex’s core offering.

The GTM strategy combines:
- High-intent search traffic
- Founder communities
- Direct outreach
- Credex’s existing customer relationships

Because the product is free and produces tangible savings estimates, the barrier to adoption is low. Even if only a small percentage of users convert into consultations, the economics are attractive because the cost to acquire each lead is minimal.
