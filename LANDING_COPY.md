## FAQ

### 1. How does StackTrim calculate savings?

StackTrim compares your current AI subscriptions against current public pricing, team size, and usage patterns to identify lower-cost plans and practical alternatives.

### 2. Which AI tools are supported?

StackTrim supports Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, Gemini, Windsurf, OpenAI API, and Anthropic API.

### 3. Do I need to connect any accounts?

No. Simply enter your current plans and monthly spend. No integrations or API keys are required.

### 4. Is my data private?

Yes. Public share links automatically remove identifying information such as company name and email address.

### 5. What happens after I submit my audit?

You receive a personalized report instantly. If the audit identifies significant savings opportunities, you can optionally connect with Credex to explore discounted AI infrastructure credits.
